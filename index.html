WHERE INNOVATION MEETS EXCELLENCE

Welcome to the Hemmes Engineering and Science Society

The official club of NYU-CAS dual degree program between the College of Arts and Science and the Tandon School of Engineering. The future of the future, a community open to all disciplines interested in the intersection between disciplines. Named in honor of Joe Hemmes, a transfer advisor and founder of NYU's dual degree program in the 1980's who unforunately passed away in 2009, our club lives on to carry his legacy and help others interested in a dual degree make their own path in this world.

How to get involved

Join us in shaping the future

Whether you're an engineer, scientist, or simply curious about the intersection of disciplines, HESS is the place for you. Join us to explore new ideas, collaborate on projects, and make a difference.
